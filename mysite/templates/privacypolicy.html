We value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information:

Information We Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, and address (for profile and pickup coordination).
  • Item details, listings, and requests.
  • Messages exchanged within the platform.

How We Use Information

  • To connect donors and receivers.
  • To notify you about requests, approvals, or messages.
  • To improve services and provide user support.

Data Privacy

  • Your personal contact details (email/phone) will not be publicly visible.
  • Location information is shared only for item pickup coordination.

Data Security

  • Passwords are encrypted before storage.
  • Data is backed up daily to avoid loss.

Sharing Information

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.
  • Data may be shared with law enforcement if required by law.

Cookies & Tracking

  • We may use cookies to improve user experience.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

User Control

  • You can update or delete your account anytime.
  • You can report content or users who misuse the platform.

Policy Updates

  • We may update our privacy policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page.
