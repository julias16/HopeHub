About Us

Our mission is to reduce waste and provide essential resources to those in need.

Share & Care is a platform dedicated to connecting individuals and organizations who have surplus items with those who need them. Whether it's excess food, clothing, or furniture, we facilitate the sharing of resources to minimize waste and support our community.
